You have the choice between two characters :

  • An innocent hamburger
  • A hungry human

The role of the hamburger is simple, get out of the maze by having picked up the most steaks

The role of the human, eat the hamburger and prevent it from escaping

Beware, the human goes faster than the hamburger, if he eats you, you lose 1 steak, if you have 0 steak in your possession and the human catches you, you lose!
